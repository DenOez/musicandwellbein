Music is an essential part of everyday life for many people.
The way people listen to music is very individual and therefore music has different purposes depending on the situation, place and time.
Music can be used as a tool to regulate and manage emotions. Listening to music has the ability to reduce stress, to create positive emotions or to increase physical performance.

Our web application is analyzing music listening habits of Spotify users. During this process the application analyses the most
recent 50 tracks played by Spotify users, related to different factors such as danceability, instrumentalness, energy, acousticness,
pitch, tempo and other indicators. With these information we intent to find correlation between music listening habits and human well-being.
